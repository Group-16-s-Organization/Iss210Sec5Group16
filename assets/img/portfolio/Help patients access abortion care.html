National Abortion Hotline

The National Abortion Hotline is the largest national, toll-free, multi-lingual Hotline for abortion provider information and financial assistance in the U.S. and Canada. We provide callers with accurate information, confidential consultation, options counseling, and information on providers of quality abortion care. We also provide case management services for patients with special needs, and limited financial assistance to help subsidize care and travel-related expenses.

Every penny we raise for our patient assistance funds goes directly to patients who need help traveling or affording travel-related expenses in order to obtain their abortion care.

You can donate to one of the following patient assistance funds to help patients access the abortion care they need:

The Rachel Falls Patient Assistance Fund helps patients obtain the abortion care they need but otherwise could not afford. The fund honors the memory of former Hotline Director Rachel Falls. By donating to this fund, 100% of your gift will go to help women obtain their abortion care.

The Dr. Tiller Patient Assistance Fund primarily helps cover travel-related expenses for patients who need to travel to obtain their abortion care. This fund honors the legacy of long-time NAF member, Dr. George Tiller who was murdered by an anti-abortion extremist in 2009. By donating to this fund, 100% of your gift will go to help patients access the safe, high-quality abortion care they need.

Gifts to the National Abortion Hotline are tax-deductible in the U.S. to the extent permitted by law. The National Abortion Hotline has a Platinum Seal of Transparency from Guidestar.
